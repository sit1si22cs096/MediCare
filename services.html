Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare services for you and your family

General Medicine

Our General Medicine department provides comprehensive care for adults, focusing on preventive care, diagnosis, and treatment of various health conditions.

Our experienced physicians take a holistic approach to healthcare, considering all aspects of your health to provide personalized treatment plans.

  • Routine check-ups and physical examinations
  • Diagnosis and treatment of common illnesses
  • Management of chronic conditions
  • Preventive healthcare and screenings
  • Health education and lifestyle counseling
  • Immunizations and vaccinations

Cardiology

Our Cardiology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of heart conditions using state-of-the-art technology and evidence-based practices.

Our team of cardiologists is dedicated to providing exceptional care for patients with cardiovascular diseases, from prevention to advanced treatment options.

  • Comprehensive cardiac evaluations
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG)
  • Echocardiography
  • Stress testing
  • Holter monitoring
  • Management of heart conditions
  • Cardiac rehabilitation programs
Book an Appointment

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics department provides specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents in a child-friendly environment designed to make them feel comfortable and at ease.

Our pediatricians are trained to address the unique healthcare needs of growing children, from routine check-ups to complex medical conditions.

  • Well-child visits and growth monitoring
  • Immunizations and vaccinations
  • Developmental assessments
  • Treatment of childhood illnesses and injuries
  • Behavioral and mental health support
  • Nutrition counseling
  • Adolescent healthcare
Book an Appointment

Orthopedics

Our Orthopedics department specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of conditions affecting the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles.

Our orthopedic surgeons and specialists provide comprehensive care for patients of all ages, from sports injuries to joint replacements and spine conditions.

  • Diagnosis and treatment of bone and joint conditions
  • Sports medicine and injury management
  • Joint replacement surgery
  • Fracture care and trauma surgery
  • Spine care and surgery
  • Physical therapy and rehabilitation
  • Minimally invasive procedures
Book an Appointment

Neurology

Our Neurology department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves.

Our neurologists use advanced diagnostic techniques and treatment approaches to address a wide range of neurological conditions, from headaches to complex neurological disorders.

  • Comprehensive neurological evaluations
  • Electroencephalogram (EEG)
  • Electromyography (EMG)
  • Treatment of headaches and migraines
  • Management of epilepsy and seizure disorders
  • Care for stroke patients
  • Treatment of neurodegenerative diseases
  • Sleep disorder diagnosis and management
Book an Appointment

Dermatology

Our Dermatology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting the skin, hair, and nails, using the latest techniques and technologies.

Our dermatologists provide comprehensive care for patients of all ages, addressing both medical and cosmetic dermatological concerns with personalized treatment plans.

  • Diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions
  • Skin cancer screening and treatment
  • Acne and rosacea management
  • Eczema and psoriasis care
  • Dermatological surgery
  • Cosmetic dermatology procedures
  • Laser treatments
  • Hair and nail disorder treatment
